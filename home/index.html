About Us

Little Angels Academy is a primary school that was founded in 2009 by the manager, Jane, and the director, Peter. Our mottoo is Strive to Excel We have three branches located in Kiganjo, Kimorori, and Kisii. Our mission is to provide quality education that goes beyond academics to instill values that will shape the character of our future leaders. We have a dedicated team of passionate teachers who are committed to creating a supportive learning environment for our students. Our modern facilities and resources enable us to deliver a holistic education that nurtures the mind, heart, and soul of our students.
